Tarihin Makarantar Baitul Mukarram

Tarihin yadda Allah ya Raini wannan makarantar tun kafin ta kafu da kafanta.

Manyan Ci gaba:

  • 2016 - Yadda aka samu Filin Makarantar
  • 2018 - Abubuwan da makarantar ta fara dasu
  • 2020 - kalubalen da makarantar ta fuskanta
  • 2023 - Tarihin yadda Makarantar ta canja tsarinta kuma tayi karfi sosai

Hotuna:

Allahu Akbar, makarantar ta samu asalin ci gabanta daga wani abu da ya tada hankalin malamai da daliban makarantar kamar yadda zakuga ga wasu malamai daga cikin malaman makarantar cike da damuwa sunzo suna kalon ikon allah, babu wani kudi da makarantar take ajiyewa domin fuskatar barazanar da zai iya tunkaranta nan gaba. Wannan yasa kowa yake kallon kamar ta gujewa duk wata barazana, sai dai Allah yakan Jarabci Bawa domin ya nuna masa wani abun da bai daukeshi hatsari a rayuwarsa ba daga qarshe sai ya gyara masa tunaninsa da ita.

Ko me zai faru dai gashinan an rusa makarantar kuma malaman sune leburorinta kamar yadda zaku ga daya daga cikin malaman ya zage yana aiki. Sai dai kash duk da zulumin da malaman ke ciki na gininsu ya rushe an qara samun wani tashin hankali lokacin maida sabon ginin. Lokacin da aka bada filin mai filin ya zana iya inda za ayi makarantar ya bar sauran a kanshi to lokacin da ya bada filin akwai wata bishiya a setin ginin to sai yace a dauki harsashin ginin daga bayan bishiyan...

An dai ci gaba da tonon harsashin ginin kamar yadda ya bayyana a wannan hoton; duk wannan ya faru ne a ranar da aka rushe ginin, sannan wannan aikin bai sa an bada hutu a dalibai ba; haka aka ci gaba da karantarwa a daya gefen.

Abu dai ya fara Tafiya Sai Godiya — gashi waje ya fara haskawa da sabon gini da aka fara; zukatan malamai ya fara samun nutsuwa duk da rashin kudin da makarantar take dashi amma akwai kyakkyawan manufa akan ci gaba. Anyi kos biyar har da harsashin ginin a rana ta farko...

Alhamdu lillahi — Gini dai ya fara kamo tsawon Mutum a rana ta biyu, wannan da kuke gani Malamine ya dauki buta za ayi sallan Azahar kafin a ci gaba...
